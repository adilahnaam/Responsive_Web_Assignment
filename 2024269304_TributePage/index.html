Tun Dr. Mahathir Mohamad

Tun Dr. Mahathir Mohamad, former Prime Minister of Malaysia.
Tun Dr. Mahathir Mohamad is one of the most influential leaders in Malaysia’s history. He served as the Prime Minister of Malaysia for two terms, from 1981 to 2003 and again from 2018 to 2020.

During his leadership, Malaysia experienced rapid development and modernization. He introduced Vision 2020, aiming to transform Malaysia into a fully developed nation. His administration was responsible for major national projects such as the Petronas Twin Towers, Putrajaya, and Cyberjaya.

He is widely known for his strong leadership style, strategic thinking, and firm decision-making. Although often controversial, his impact on Malaysia’s economic growth and infrastructure development is undeniable.

Today, he remains a significant figure in Malaysian political history and is remembered as a leader who shaped the modern identity of the country.
